Frequently Asked Questions

1. What kind of applications do you build?

We build cross-platform mobile applications (iOS and Android), progressive web apps (PWAs), and complex web platforms with a focus on modern UI/UX and advanced AI integration.

2. How long does it take to develop an app?

The timeline heavily depends on the scope and complexity of the project. A standard MVP (Minimum Viable Product) typically takes 4 to 8 weeks. Larger, enterprise-grade applications can take several months. Our agile workflow ensures you visually see progress quickly.

3. Do you provide post-launch support and maintenance?

Yes. We offer continuous maintenance packages to ensure your application stays completely up-to-date with the latest OS versions, security patches, and frontend performance optimizations.

4. How much does a custom app cost?

Pricing varies significantly based on features, platforms supported, and third-party API integrations necessary. We provide an exact, upfront estimate after our initial technical discovery phase. Please use the "Request a Quote" option in our main contact form to get started.

5. Do you handle App Store & Google Play publishing?

Absolutely. We completely manage the entire submission lifecycle, including metadata, optimized screenshots, and ensuring strict compliance with all evolving app store guidelines.
